Hello and Welcome!

2021-08-20 2 min read Life

Finally creating my own site!

After almost a year of deciding to create a personal website, it has finally come to fruition! After many months of playing around with various website generator platforms (notably Wordpress and Squarespace), I decided to do a 180 and use R Blogdown.

There are several reasons for this:

  • Money: most website generator platforms cost between $6-50 per month (for a basic subscription). As a (sort of) student, I want to cut costs where I can.
  • Skill: While I am currently learning how to code HTML (slowly), I am nowhere near the skill necessary to code an entire personal website. That said, while I’m no HTML wiz, I do consider myself quite the R wizard. With a bit of research, a lot of patience and some learning I was able to put together this website. (with many thanks to the various R forums and tutorials on the internet)
  • Practicality: For the foreseeable future, I plan to be in the field of economic research or applied economics. After seeing many of my mentors and fellow economists develop similar websites, I wanted a platform suitable for research and academia Low and behold, the Hugo Academic theme! I found many website platforms catered to website platforms for companies and online shops, which is unfortunately unideal for my circumstances.

As I continue developing my programming skills and contribute more to my website, I hope to refine certain elements and customize my site further. Which leads me to my next topic, what is this site for?

Using this website to share my research, projects, and day-to-day life.

As a young economist and professional, I want a platform where I can share my research and interests with fellow economists and friends. That said, I am currently at a point in my career where I am exploring fields, economic areas and interests. Having completed my degree from UofT this year, I now have no such setting to “take courses” and learn new material. Therefore, I am taking it into my own hands and using this platform to share new research I develop and, admittedly, hold myself accountable.

My goal is to create a new journal posts at least bi-weekly regarding either personal reflection, economic research or an op-ed on current topics.

Aug-2021 Life
